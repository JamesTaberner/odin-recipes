Butter Chicken

Description

Butter Chicken is a classic Indian dish that is a staple in many households. It is a hearty and flavorful dish that is perfect for a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast - 500g
  • Yoghurt - 200g
  • Carrot - 1
  • Brown Onion - 1
  • Butter Chicken Packet - 1
  • Sweet Potato - 1
  • Tinned Tomatoes - 800g
  • Beef Stock - 500ml

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add the chicken breast.
  2. Cook until browned and set aside.
  3. Add the onion, carrot, and sweet potato to the pan and cook until softened.
  4. Return the chicken to the pan and add the butter chicken packet.
  5. Stir in the tinned tomatoes and beef stock.
  6. Simmer for 1-2 hours until thickened.
  7. Serve over cooked rice.
