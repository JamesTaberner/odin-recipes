Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian dish that is a staple in many households. It is a hearty and flavorful dish that is perfect for a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • Beef Mince - 500g
  • Tomato Paste - 7g
  • Carrot - 1
  • Brown Onion - 1
  • Celery Stalks - 2
  • Red Wine - 150ml
  • Tinned Tomatoes - 800g
  • Beef Stock - 500ml

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add the beef mince.
  2. Cook until browned and set aside.
  3. Add the onion, carrot, and celery to the pan and cook until softened.
  4. Return the beef to the pan and add the tomato paste.
  5. Stir in the red wine and cook until reduced.
  6. Add the tinned tomatoes and beef stock.
  7. Simmer for 1-2 hours until thickened.
  8. Serve over cooked spaghetti.
