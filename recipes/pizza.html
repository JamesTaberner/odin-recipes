Sicilian Pizza

Description

Sicilian Pizza is a rectangular sheet tray pizza which creates a foccocia style dough and toppings with the cheese on the bottom.

Ingredients

  • Bread Flour - 500g
  • Instant Yeast - 7g
  • Cooking Salt - 14g
  • Olive Oil - 60g
  • Room Temp. Water - 325g
  • Low Moisture Motzeralla - 500g
  • Tinned Tomatoes - 800g
  • Pepperoni - 300g

Steps

  1. Combine flour, yeast, salt, olive oil, and water in a bowl.
  2. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Let the dough rest for 15 minutes.
  4. Stretch the dough to fit a sheet tray.
  5. Top with cheese, tomatoes, and pepperoni.
  6. Bake at 500 degrees for 15 minutes.
